History

Faith Unity Conference began in 2008 as a small prayer gathering of 30 believers in our local community center. What started as a weekend retreat for spiritual renewal has grown into an annual conference that draws thousands of participants from across the region and beyond.

Over the years, our conference has expanded to include powerful worship sessions, transformative teaching seminars, mission outreach programs, and community service projects. We've witnessed countless testimonies of healing, restoration, and spiritual breakthrough.

By 2020, we adapted to reach even more hearts through livestreamed services and virtual prayer groups, connecting believers worldwide. Today, Faith Unity Conference continues to be a beacon of hope and spiritual transformation in our community.

Mission

Our mission at Faith Unity Conference is to strengthen believers, spread God's love, and build a community rooted in faith, hope, and service. We believe in the power of coming together to worship, learn, and grow in our relationship with Christ.

We are committed to:

  • Providing inspiring worship experiences and biblical teaching
  • Supporting local missions and community outreach programs
  • Fostering fellowship and spiritual growth among all ages
  • Equipping believers to share their faith and serve others

Past Speakers

Faith Unity Conference has been blessed to host inspiring pastors, evangelists, and Christian leaders who have touched countless lives. Here are some of our notable past speakers:

Pastor Michael Johnson

Senior Pastor & International Speaker

Pastor Johnson has led congregations for over 25 years and is known for his powerful messages on faith and perseverance. His 2022 keynote "Walking in God's Purpose" brought healing and direction to hundreds of attendees seeking God's will for their lives.

Evangelist Maria Santos

Missionary & Youth Minister

With over 15 years of mission work across three continents, Evangelist Santos has dedicated her life to sharing the Gospel. Her 2023 session "Called to Serve" inspired dozens of young people to answer the call to missions and community service.

Dr. David Thompson

Biblical Scholar & Author

Dr. Thompson's deep knowledge of Scripture and gift for teaching have made him a beloved conference speaker. His 2021 series on "Living the Beatitudes Today" provided practical wisdom for Christian living and remains one of our most requested teaching resources.
